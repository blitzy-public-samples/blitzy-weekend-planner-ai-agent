# Getting Started with Weekend Planner

Welcome to Weekend Planner! We're excited to help you discover amazing activities for your next family weekend. This guide will walk you through everything you need to get started.

---

## What is Weekend Planner?

Weekend Planner is your personal AI assistant for planning family weekend activities. Simply tell it where you are and who's coming along, and it will:

- **Check the weather** for your area to make smart recommendations
- **Find local activities** perfect for your family
- **Discover special events** like festivals, fairs, and community gatherings
- **Suggest indoor alternatives** when the weather isn't cooperating
- **Tailor suggestions** to your children's ages

## What You'll Need

Before you begin, make sure you have:

### A Web Browser

Any modern web browser will work great:
- Google Chrome
- Mozilla Firefox
- Apple Safari
- Microsoft Edge

### The Application Running

The Weekend Planner needs to be running on your computer. When everything is set up correctly, you'll access it by visiting **localhost:5173** in your web browser.

> **Note:** If you're not sure how to get the application running, ask your technical contact for help with the initial setup.

## Your First Weekend Plan

Ready to plan your perfect weekend? Follow these simple steps:

### Step 1: Open the Application

Open your web browser and go to **localhost:5173**. You'll see the Weekend Planner home screen with a form on the left side waiting for your input.

![See home-empty-state.png for the initial screen](images/home-empty-state.png.txt)

### Step 2: Enter Your Location

In the **Location** field, type where you'll be spending your weekend. You can enter:
- A city name (like "San Francisco" or "Austin, Texas")
- A zip code (like "90210" or "02138")

This helps Weekend Planner find activities near you and check your local weather.

### Step 3: Select Your Weekend Dates

Choose when your weekend adventure will take place:

1. Click on the **Start Date** field and select the first day of your weekend
2. Click on the **End Date** field and select the last day of your weekend

> **Tip:** The end date must be on or after your start date.

### Step 4: Add Your Children's Ages (Optional)

If you have children joining your weekend activities, enter their ages in the **Kids Ages** field. Separate multiple ages with commas.

**Examples:**
- For one child: 7
- For multiple children: 3, 7, 12
- For twins and an older sibling: 4, 4, 9

This helps Weekend Planner suggest age-appropriate activities that everyone will enjoy.

### Step 5: Share Your Preferences (Optional)

Have specific interests or requirements? Type them in the **Preferences** field. 

**Ideas for what to include:**
- Favorite types of activities ("outdoor adventures", "art and crafts")
- Things to avoid ("avoid crowds", "nothing too strenuous")
- Special interests ("loves dinosaurs", "enjoys swimming")
- Budget considerations ("free activities", "budget-friendly")

### Step 6: Generate Your Plan

Once you've filled in at least your location and dates, the **Generate Plan** button will become active. Click it to start the magic!

![See form-filled.png for a completed form example](images/form-filled.png.txt)

### Step 7: Wait for Your Personalized Plan

Weekend Planner will now work on creating your custom weekend itinerary. You'll see a loading screen while the AI researches activities for you.

![See loading-state.png for the loading screen](images/loading-state.png.txt)

This usually takes just a few moments. The AI is:
- Checking the weather forecast for your area
- Searching for local activities and events
- Finding age-appropriate options for your children
- Putting together personalized recommendations

### Step 8: Review Your Weekend Suggestions

Once complete, you'll see your personalized weekend plan! Browse through the activity suggestions and start getting excited about your upcoming adventure.

![See plan-result.png for a sample plan](images/plan-result.png.txt)

## Understanding Your Results

Weekend Planner provides you with thoughtfully curated suggestions. Here's what to expect:

### Weather-Aware Recommendations

Your plan takes the weather into account automatically:

- **Good weather**: You'll get suggestions for outdoor activities and local events
- **Bad weather**: Expect a mix of indoor and covered outdoor options
- **Severe weather**: Focus shifts to safe, fun at-home activities

The AI checks real forecasts for your area, so recommendations make sense for what's actually expected.

### Activity Categories

Your results may include several types of activities:

| Category | What to Expect |
|----------|----------------|
| **Local Activities** | Nearby parks, attractions, and family-friendly venues |
| **Special Events** | Festivals, fairs, concerts, and community gatherings happening during your weekend |
| **Home Activities** | Creative indoor projects, crafts, and games (especially when weather is poor) |

### Viewing Detailed Information

Want to see exactly what the AI found? Click the **"Show Raw Output"** option to expand the detailed information panel. This shows you the complete response from the AI, including all the research that went into your recommendations.

![See plan-raw-output.png for the expanded raw output view](images/plan-raw-output.png.txt)

### Important Note About Results

The suggestions you receive are based on AI research and should be verified for accuracy and availability. We recommend:

- Checking venue websites or calling ahead to confirm hours and availability
- Looking up current pricing, as it may have changed
- Verifying that events are still scheduled as planned

## Starting Over

Want to plan a different weekend? Simply click the **Reset** button to clear the form and results, then start fresh with new information.

---

## Need to Make Changes?

If you want to adjust your plan:

1. Update any of the fields in the form on the left
2. Click **Generate Plan** again
3. A new, updated plan will be created based on your changes

Feel free to experiment with different dates, locations, or preferences to explore all your options!
